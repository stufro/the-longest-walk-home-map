The Longest Walk Home

by Ray Bailey with David Wilkins

The Longest Walk Home is the incredible account of his daring 2,000 mile escape across Europe and over the Pyrenees, to the safety of British Gibraltar, and home in time for Christmas.

About this map

I loved reading this book. I wanted to follow along on a map but couldn't find one online, so decided to create this myself.

DISCLAIMER: I have almost certainly made mistakes. I made this by scanning the book a 2nd time and jotting down any place names. If you spot any issues, feel free to contact me through my site, or if you have an account, raise a GitHub issue.
